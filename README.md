Project Description

The Carbon Emission Tracker is a digital platform designed to monitor, analyze, and visualize carbon emissions generated by individuals, organizations, or specific activities. By integrating data from energy consumption, transportation, and industrial sources, the system provides real-time insights into carbon footprints. It empowers users to make data-driven decisions, adopt sustainable practices, and comply with environmental standards. The platform features dashboards, alerts, and recommendations, ensuring transparency and promoting eco-friendly behavior.

Problem Statement

With rising concerns about climate change, organizations and individuals often lack a clear understanding of their carbon emissions and their impact on the environment. Existing tools are either too complex, fragmented, or inaccessible for real-time monitoring and actionable insights. Without accurate tracking, it becomes difficult to identify emission hotspots, set reduction targets, or measure progress toward sustainability goals. There is a need for a simple, reliable, and secure system that integrates multiple data sources to provide clear visibility into carbon emissions.

Expected Outcomes

- A functional system to track carbon emissions from multiple sources in real-time.
- Data visualization dashboards with key metrics (emission levels, trends, hotspots, comparisons).
- Alerts and notifications when emissions exceed thresholds.
- Actionable recommendations to reduce emissions and improve sustainability practices.
- Enhanced awareness and accountability among users toward reducing their carbon footprint.
- Contribution to global sustainability goals by enabling data-driven decisions.
